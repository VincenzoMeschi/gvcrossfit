Location

GV Crossfit is located at 1000 68th St. Suite 100, Allendale, MI 49401. We are located in the same building as the Allendale Township Hall. We are on the second floor, so please use the stairs or elevator to get to the second floor. We are located in the back of the building, so please enter through the back door.
