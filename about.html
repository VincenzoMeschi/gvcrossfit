What is Crossfit?

Crossfit is a combination of High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT), weightlifting, powerlifting, endurance training, and gymnastics specifically designed to cater towards those interested in a rigorous workout.

Here at GV Crossfit, we follow a four step routine in all of our classes.

Warm Up

We begin every workout with a light cardio warmup of either jogging or cycling. After cardio, the class is led in a low resistance movement session to warm up muscles and improve range of motion for the remainder of the workout.

Movement sessions can include anything from yoga to resistance training that incorporate weights, resistance bands, and machines.

Weightlifting

The next step of our Crossfit classes is weightlifting. Weight and duration varies per class and level, but is mainly free weight dumbbells, kettlebells, and medicine balls.

Metabolic Conditioning

The third activity in our classes is the daily workout. Daily workouts vary per class, level, and instructor and can include anything from box jumping, to powerlifting, and agility training. Be sure to check the daily schedule posted in the gym for immediate updates!

Cooldown

For a functional cool down, instructors will lead the class through a deep breathing and free flow movement segment lasting roughly five minutes at the end of each class.
