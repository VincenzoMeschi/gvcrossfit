Classes

We offer a series of classes focusing on High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) and strength training designed to help you become your best self.

Here at CrossFit, we know that in order to continue to see growth in your fitness journey, it’s important to up your intensity in a safe and controlled environment. This is why we have designed our gradual progression class program to grow with you.

Level 1

Anthony | Beginner

Meet Anthony, our beginners coach.

Anthony’s classes are designed for our customers with little to no previous experience in the gym who are looking to begin their fitness journey with CrossFit.

Offered 5 days a week, Monday through Friday from 6-7am or 5-6pm in training room B, Anthony’s beginners class could be the perfect fit for you. Sign up today for 30% off your first week of classes.

Level 2

Casey | Intermediate

Meet Casey, our coach for intermediate level classes.

Casey’s classes are expertly designed to continue your fitness journey here at CrossFit!

Our level 2 classes with Casey are offered Tuesday through Saturday 7:30-8:30am or 6:30-7:30pm in training room B. Sign up today or start your journey with our beginners class for 30% off your first week of classes.

Level 3

Xander | Advanced

Meet Xander, our coach for our experienced level classes.

Xander runs his classes on a tight schedule from 5-6am or 6-7pm in training room A. Experienced classes are offered Monday through Friday. We recommend Xander’s classes for those who are long term committed to CrossFit and have been with us since the beginning.

Blending High Intensity Interval Training with rigorous weight training, Xander’s level 3 classes are guaranteed to elevate your fitness journey to the next step. Sign up today for our beginner, intermediate, or experienced classes for 30% off your first week of classes.

Level 4

Kai | Advanced

Meet Kai, our coach for our advanced level classes.

Kai runs their classes long, believing in the power of interval training and prolonged periods of rest to recuperate and prepare for your next class. Offered Monday, Wednesday, and Friday, from 7-8:30am or 4-5:30pm, Kai’s advanced level classes are designed only for the most experienced CrossFit trainees.

Included in the enrollment for Kai’s advanced level 4 classes is their book, Beyond by Coach Kai Westover where they talk more about reaping the benefits of CrossFit, incorporating the CrossFit lifestyle into your everyday life, and nutrition tips from our on site nutritionist, Bobby Moore.

Sign up today for your free copy of Beyond by Kai Westover as well as 30% off your first week of classes.
